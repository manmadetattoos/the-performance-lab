FAQ

Common questions.

Do you need my passwords or login details?

No. We use Meta's Business Manager partner access and Google Ads manager account access — both are read-only and can be revoked at any time. Your account remains fully under your control.

What if my accounts are small or I've just started?

Audits are valuable at any stage. In early accounts we focus on structural issues and tracking integrity; in mature accounts we focus on efficiency and waste. We'll tell you upfront if an audit isn't the right fit.

Is this a sales pitch for an ongoing retainer?

No. The audit is a standalone deliverable. We do offer retainer services if you want ongoing support, but there is zero obligation — you'll never be pressured into anything.

How is this different from what my agency already does?

Agencies have an inherent conflict of interest — their revenue depends on you staying. We're independent. We have no reason to hide problems or downplay waste, because our only job is to find it.

What do I receive at the end?

A written audit report covering every issue found, ranked by impact, with specific recommended actions. It's designed to be actionable — whether you implement the changes yourself or hand it to your team or agency.
